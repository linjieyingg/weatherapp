Welcome to Rain Rain Go Away, your personal weather search engine. Please follow the following instructions to use the website:
  1. Please login to access the website and save your searches.
  2. Look up a weather location using the search bar.
  3. Navigate to "Daily Weather" to view the 3-days weather condition for your searched location. You may also click on the individual dates to view the detail weather conditions for that day.
  4. Navigate to "Hourly Weather" to view the 24-hours weather condition for your searched location. You may also click on the individual hours to view the detail weather conditions for that hour.
  5. In the daily weather page, you may add any notes you have for the day, such as your mood, to-do, etc.
